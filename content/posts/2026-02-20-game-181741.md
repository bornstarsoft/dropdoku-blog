Frogdoku, the beloved logic puzzle game, is back in the spotlight with its latest free update, Konnas Winter Wonderland. Launching on National Comfy Day, February 20, 2026, this update invites players to enjoy 15 new handcrafted levels that embody the essence of cozy gaming. With its gentle approach to logic puzzles, Frogdoku continues to provide a stress-free environment for players to unwind and engage their minds.

The winter-themed levels are adorned with snow-covered tiles and charming environmental details, creating a serene atmosphere perfect for mindful play. Accompanied by three new seasonal music tracks, players can immerse themselves in a tranquil experience as they guide Konna, the protagonist frog, through delightful puzzles dressed in adorable winter outfits. This update not only enhances the gameplay but also reinforces the community spirit of Frogdoku, making it a heartfelt gift for fans.

As we celebrate this cozy addition to the Frogdoku universe, it raises an exciting question: what other seasonal updates can we look forward to in the future? The warmth of Konnas Winter Wonderland is just the beginning of a series of free releases that promise to keep the cozy puzzle love alive.
