Namco's 1994 arcade game, Cyber Commando, is finally making its way to home consoles, marking a significant moment for retro gaming enthusiasts. Previously exclusive to Japanese arcades, this competitive vehicle shooter is the sequel to Cyber Sled and offers players the chance to engage in thrilling battles in a futuristic setting on Uranus. With its unique gameplay mechanics and nostalgic appeal, Cyber Commando is set to captivate a new generation of gamers.

Developed by Hamster Corporation, the home release will be available on multiple platforms, including Switch, PS5, and Xbox Series X/S, as part of their Arcade Archives series. Players can expect various modes, including Original Mode and High Score Mode, along with modern enhancements like multiple save slots and a rewind feature. This release not only honors the original arcade experience but also introduces new players to the excitement of classic gaming.

As we look forward to its release on April 30, one can't help but wonder: how will Cyber Commando resonate with today's gaming audience, and will it inspire a revival of interest in other forgotten gems from the arcade era?
