If youre a cat lover and enjoy puzzle games, then Kitty Haven is a title you wont want to miss. This upcoming cozy puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with unique personalities and needs. From picky eaters to feisty furballs, the game challenges you to arrange spaces that cater to their whims while solving engaging puzzles.

Kitty Haven not only allows you to unlock new items and residents but also encourages creativity in decorating your own haven. The demo is currently available on Steam, giving players a taste of the delightful challenges that await. As the gaming community eagerly anticipates its full release, one cant help but wonder how this charming game will resonate with fans of both cats and casual gaming.
