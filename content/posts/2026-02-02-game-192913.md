If youre a PlayStation Plus subscriber, theres a hidden gem waiting for you: The Exit 8, a free puzzle game that has recently joined the lineup. This indie title, developed by Kotake Create, offers a unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving that is sure to keep you on your toes. Set in a hauntingly atmospheric Japanese metro tunnel, players must identify various anomalies to escape, making it a thrilling experience that stands out among the new additions to PS Plus.

The gameplay revolves around walking through a looped environment, where players encounter both subtle and overt anomalies. From mismatched floor tiles to eerie images appearing on the walls, the game cleverly plays with your perception, creating a sense of unease. Completing the game requires players to identify these anomalies correctly eight times in a row, adding a layer of challenge that is both engaging and rewarding.

While The Exit 8 may not be a full-fledged horror game, its creepy elements and intriguing mechanics make it a worthwhile experience for those looking for something different.
