Apple Arcade has just enriched its library with two indie masterpieces: Dredge and Unpacking. Both games have been meticulously optimized for mobile devices, ensuring that players can enjoy these unique experiences on the go. Dredge+ offers a chilling blend of fishing and horror, while Unpacking+ transforms the simple act of unpacking into a heartfelt puzzle game.

Dredge+ stands out with its intriguing gameplay that combines fishing mechanics with a narrative steeped in Lovecraftian horror. Players navigate the waters by day, catching fish and managing their catch, only to face nightmarish creatures after sunset. This duality creates a gripping atmosphere that keeps players on edge, reminiscent of classic horror games like Eternal Darkness.

On the other hand, Unpacking+ takes a more serene approach. This game invites players to arrange household items in a new home, making it a perfect fit for short gaming sessions. Its touching narrative has earned it accolades, including recognition for Cultural Impact at the 2023 App Store Awards. 

With these additions, Apple Arcade continues to solidify its reputation as a haven for indie game lovers. Are you ready to dive into these captivating worlds?
