Hello Kitty creator Sanrio is making waves by launching a new gaming brand, Sanrio Games, to tap into the booming global gaming market. With plans to release ten titles over the next three years, the company aims to leverage its beloved characters in a party game format for Nintendo Switch and Switch 2. This strategic move highlights a growing trend among Japanese IP owners to diversify revenue streams through interactive entertainment.

Sanrio's CEO, Tomokuni Tsuji, emphasized the potential of gaming to expand their character portfolio into the North American market, which could significantly enhance their licensing business. While the standalone gaming venture is expected to incur initial losses, the investment of approximately 10 billion yen signals a strong commitment to this new direction. The success of these titles will depend on Sanrio's ability to create engaging experiences that resonate with both new and existing fans.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, it will be fascinating to see how Sanrio's characters adapt to this interactive medium. Will they capture the hearts of gamers just as they have with fans around the world? Only time will tell.
