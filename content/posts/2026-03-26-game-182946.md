Toukana Interactive has just announced an exciting new chapter for their beloved puzzle game, Dorfromantik. The upcoming Medieval Biome Pack promises to transform the games serene landscapes into a charming medieval world, complete with historical makeovers for iconic tiles. Players can look forward to seeing their steam boats replaced with majestic medieval trading cogs and castle ruins rebuilt into formidable fortresses.

In addition to the paid DLC, a free Anniversary Update will also be rolled out, introducing integrated leaderboards and a much-anticipated Night Mode. This new visual feature will enhance the gaming experience, making it more comfortable for evening play with its soothing blue tones and warm glowing windows. As Dorfromantik celebrates its 5th anniversary, these updates are sure to delight both new and veteran players alike.

With both the Medieval Biome Pack and the Anniversary Update set for release in May/June 2026, fans are eagerly awaiting more details. How do you think these new features will change the way we play Dorfromantik?
