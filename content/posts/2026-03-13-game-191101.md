The excitement is building as the pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, gears up for its launch this June on PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC via Steam. Developed by Smitner Studio and published by Numskull Games, this game promises to deliver "ingenious, compact, handcrafted" puzzle levels that are sure to challenge players' problem-solving skills and creativity.

For those eager to get a taste of the gameplay, a demo is currently available for PC users, allowing fans to dive into the unique mechanics and charming visuals that Hazard Pay has to offer. With its retro aesthetic and engaging puzzles, this title is poised to capture the hearts of both casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike.

As we await the official release, one can only wonder how this game will stack up against other indie titles in the genre. Will Hazard Pay redefine the block-pushing puzzle game experience? Only time will tell!
