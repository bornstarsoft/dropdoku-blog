In a world where memories shape destinies, the new game Hannah VCR invites players to manipulate the narrative itself. This innovative puzzle game allows you to change the order of scenes in a VHS-like format, guiding the protagonist, Hannah, through a haunting journey filled with emotional depth and mystery. With its retro aesthetic and immersive soundtrack, Hannah VCR offers a unique experience that challenges players to consider the impact of their choices on the unfolding story.

Available on Xbox Series X|S and PC for just 9.99, Hannah VCR is not only a testament to creative storytelling but also showcases the power of Xbox Play Anywhere, allowing seamless gameplay across devices. As you dive into this emotionally charged adventure, youll find yourself pondering: can you find the right sequence to save Hannah and change her destiny?
