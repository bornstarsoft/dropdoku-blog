Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has made waves in the gaming community by being awarded the title of Best Puzzle Game at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025. This accolade is not just a trophy on the shelf; it reflects the game's impressive download velocity and user retention, showcasing how players around the globe have embraced its unique gameplay mechanics. With its intuitive drag-match-disappear feature, Block Blast! has successfully captured the hearts of players in over 200 countries, ranking No. 1 in more than 40 markets.

In 2025, Hungry Studio's commitment to localized strategies in the APAC region has paid off, with initiatives like prefecture-based high-score challenges in Japan and collaborations with top-tier celebrities in Korea. These efforts have transformed Block Blast! from a global sensation into a beloved local favorite, seamlessly integrating into the daily lives of diverse players. As Jason Wang, VP of Brand Marketing at Hungry Studio, noted, this recognition inspires the team to continue innovating and listening to their player community.

As we look ahead, the success of Block Blast! raises an intriguing question: what will be the next big trend in mobile gaming? With the gaming landscape constantly evolving, it will be exciting to see how Hungry Studio and others adapt to meet the demands of players worldwide.
