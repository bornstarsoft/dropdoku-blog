The newly launched puzzle game AiliA offers a captivating blend of reality and reflection, inviting players to dive into a world where mirrors are not just for show but are integral to gameplay. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, this 3D visual puzzle game challenges players to manipulate mirrors to solve over 100 intricate puzzles, all while uncovering the story of sisters Aria and Lia.

AiliA stands out with its innovative mirror mechanics that transform the environment, allowing players to explore both real and mirrored worlds. With a launch price of $11.99 and a 20% discount for the first two weeks, plus a special bundle deal with Moncage, its an enticing offer for puzzle enthusiasts. A free demo is also available on Steam, giving players a taste of this unique gameplay experience.

As you navigate through seven beautifully themed areas, the game promises not just puzzles but also engaging boss encounters that utilize its mirror mechanics. Will you be able to unlock the secrets hidden within the reflections? Dive into AiliA and find out!
