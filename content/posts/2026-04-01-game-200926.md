Dinosaur Polo Club has returned to its roots with the release of RTFM (Read The F*cking Manual), a bite-sized co-op puzzle game that showcases the studio's creative spirit and commitment to innovation. Released on April Fool's Day, this experimental game invites players to navigate the absurdities of a fictional corporate environment while solving puzzles that require both cooperation and cunning. The game is available for free on PC and Mac via itch.io, making it accessible to a wide audience eager for unique gaming experiences.

The essence of RTFM lies in its gameplay mechanics, where players take on the roles of Troubleshooter and Operator, working together to decode puzzles while managing the threat of a ghostly presence. This dynamic not only fosters teamwork but also introduces an element of social sleuthing, reminiscent of popular titles like Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes. As players navigate through the challenges, they must balance trust and deception, leading to multiple endings based on their choices.

As we look forward to more innovative projects from Dinosaur Polo Club, RTFM serves as a reminder of the power of creativity in game development. Will we see more bite-sized gems emerge from their future Creativity Weeks?
