Konami has officially announced "Castlevania: Belmonts Curse," a thrilling new installment set to launch in 2026, marking the 40th anniversary of the beloved franchise. This 2D action game, developed in collaboration with the teams behind "Dead Cells" and "The Rogue Prince of Persia," promises to deliver a nostalgic yet fresh experience for both longtime fans and newcomers alike.

Set against the backdrop of 1499 Paris, players will step into the shoes of Trevor Belmonts successor, navigating through a city besieged by monstrous creatures. The return of the iconic Vampire Killer whip, along with a variety of weapons and abilities, is sure to excite fans of the series. Konami has hinted at colorful characters, powerful foes, and hidden treasures, ensuring that exploration and combat remain at the heart of the gameplay.

As we look forward to its release on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC, the anticipation builds. Will "Belmonts Curse" redefine the action genre while honoring its roots? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain: the legacy of Castlevania continues to thrive.
