Cursed Words, the upcoming roguelike puzzle game from Forlife Interactive and Buried Things, is set to release on April 2, 2026, and it promises to bring a fresh twist to the gaming landscape. Drawing inspiration from the popular poker-like deckbuilder Belatro, this game combines word creation with quirky mechanics that will keep players engaged and entertained.

In Cursed Words, players will utilize letter tiles to form words, reminiscent of classic games like Scrabble and Boggle. However, the game introduces a unique flair by incorporating bizarre elements such as numbers and chess pieces, making the gameplay both unpredictable and fun. With 11 different characters, each boasting unique powers and synergies, players can expect a plethora of combinations and strategies to explore.

As we eagerly await the release, the excitement builds around the free demo available on Steam, allowing players to get a taste of this innovative game. Will Cursed Words carve out its niche in the gaming world, or will it be another fleeting trend? Only time will tell.
