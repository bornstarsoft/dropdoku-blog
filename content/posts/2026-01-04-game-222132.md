The recent release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console game marks a significant milestone for fans of NASCAR gaming, especially with its impressive array of base paint schemes. This game not only brings back beloved designs but also introduces new ones from the ARCA Menards Series, enhancing the overall experience for players who appreciate the aesthetics of their favorite cars.

While some notable paint schemes and sponsors are missing from the lineup, such as Monster Energy and certain alcoholic beverage brands, the inclusion of fan favorites like Red Bull is a welcome addition. This is particularly exciting as it marks Red Bull's return to NASCAR gaming after a long hiatus since 2011. The diversity in paint schemes reflects the game's commitment to providing a rich and engaging experience for players.
